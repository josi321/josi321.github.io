Sunny Morning Pho on Spadina

April 22, 2026

Took my sister there for a quick lunch on a Tuesday. It’s a narrow place, maybe 18 seats, and the broth is the reason to go. They simmer it overnight and you can taste the difference between this and the chain pho places in the same neighborhood. The brisket was clean. The condiment plate was generous.

One thing to know: they only take cash or e-transfer. Walked in expecting tap and had to find an ATM around the corner.

Yes I’d go back. Probably weekly if I worked downtown.
